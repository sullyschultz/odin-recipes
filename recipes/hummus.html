Hummus

Creamy hummus with pitabread

Super easy and quick hummus full of flavour

Server with lightly toasted pitabread

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Tahini
  • Garlic
  • Lemon
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Pitabread
  • Pine nuts

Steps

  1. Rinse Chickpeas
  2. Peel Garlic
  3. Mix Chickpeas and Garlic in a blender
  4. Add olive oil and lemon juice
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste
  6. Slightly toast the pitabread
  7. Top the creamy hummus with pine nuts
